Failure Diagnosis & Fixes:
We observed that standard RL training on SLMs causes sparse reward collapse. CRAFT integrates three main fixes: (A) Step-Level Symbolic Math Evaluator & Logical Entailment Cross-Encoder, (B) Contrastive Step-Level Preference DPO learning from reasoning path pairs, and (C) Active curriculum starting in the model's 40-70% difficulty zone and expanding on stability.
